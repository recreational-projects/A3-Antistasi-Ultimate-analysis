    About this site

    • Source code for this site; changelog; raise a bug, question or feature request

    • Version 0.9.0

    1. Total military objectives = sum(airports, sea/riverports, bases, outposts, factories, resources) ↩

    2. Known issue: towns aren't counted (and total War Level points can't be calculated) if they aren't explicitly declared in the mission files.
      Missing towns counts will be added in future ↩

    3. Ratio of mission's total War Level Points to the largest known value.
      Total War Level points = sum(8 × airports, 6 × bases, 4 × sea/riverports, 2 × outposts, 2 × factories, 2 × resources, towns) - thanks to Syrreal on AU Community Discord for pointing this out ↩
